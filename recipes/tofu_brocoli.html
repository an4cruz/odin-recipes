Sautéed vegetables with tofu

Description

I wanted to create a simple recipe for this lunch, I checked out what I had in the fridge and I created this mix, that goes well with peas(that i already had in the fridge, just added vegan cheese to it).

Ingredients

  • 200g of tofu (I used the paprika one) cut into cubes
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped ail
  • 1/2 broccoli cut in small parts
  • 1 carrot cut in slices
  • 1 vegetable broth

Steps

  1. Put Olive oil in a frying pan (in medium/high heat) and add the onions and ail, let it become golden
  2. Add the vegetable broth and 50ml of water, the broccoli and the carrot
  3. Put the lid on the pan and let it simmer for 5 minutes (medium heat)
  4. Add the tofu and mix it with a spoon, let it simmer for 5 minutes more
  5. Turn off the heat
  6. Serve it with peas and cheese or whatever you like
